Courses We Offer


At AAV High School, we are dedicated to providing a well-rounded and challenging curriculum that prepares students for success both in and outside the classroom.




Intermediate

At AAV High School, we strive to provide a strong academic foundation for all of our students, especially at the intermediate level. Our curriculum is designed to build essential skills, foster curiosity, and prepare students for the more advanced learning ahead. Our intermediate courses encourage exploration, creativity, and collaboration in a welcoming and supportive environment.

Degree

At AAV High School, we are dedicated to preparing our students for higher education and the workforce through a diverse and challenging curriculum. Our Degree/Diploma Program offers students the opportunity to explore various fields of study while developing the critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills necessary for success in the modern world. Foreign Languages – Choose from Spanish, French, or Mandarin to deepen your linguistic abilities, focusing on fluency, cultural understanding, and conversational skills.

Post Graduate

At AAV High School, we recognize the importance of preparing our students for success beyond high school. Our Post-Graduate Program is designed for those who seek to strengthen their academic foundation, refine their skills, and gain the tools necessary for higher education, vocational training, or entering the workforce. We offer a comprehensive set of courses, support services, and opportunities to ensure students are well-prepared for their next chapter.

Healthy Food

Healthy food is the foundation of a balanced lifestyle, providing the essential nutrients our bodies need to function at their best. It’s not just about what we eat, but how we fuel our bodies to stay energized, strong, and well. Healthy food includes a variety of whole, minimally processed ingredients—fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats.
